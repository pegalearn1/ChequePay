Discover Features

Cheque Pay boasts a user-friendly design, coupled with the following exceptional features

Print Cheques in Multiple Languages

Easily print cheques in a range of languages, including English, Hindi, Gujarati, Spanish, Indonesian, and more with Cheque Pay. We're dedicated to expanding our language options, and you can also make a request for additional languages.

Multiple Companies in Single Account

Manage multiple companies within a single account with Cheque Pay. Effectively organize bank accounts and chequebooks on a per-company basis for streamlined financial management.

Multi Currency

Cheque Pay supports nearly all world currencies. Set a default currency for amount-to-word conversion, and easily modify it directly from the header as needed.

User Friendly Design

Stay engaged throughout the entire process, from layout creation to final print, with Cheque Pay's instant live preview feature. Import and crop cheque images for quicker layout creation, enhancing the overall efficiency of the process.

Typo Error Free

Benefit from Cheque Pay's Auto Amount to Word converter, multiple date formats, and A/c Payee Adjuster, which prevent common mistakes during cheque writing. This tool ensures error-free writing, eliminating smudges and avoiding overwriting for a seamless experience.

Supports Most Printers

Built on the principle of resource optimization, Cheque Pay allows users to utilize any existing printer in their office. It offers broad compatibility, supporting various printer types including Laser Printers, Inkjet Printers, Deskjet Printers, and All-In-One Printers.
